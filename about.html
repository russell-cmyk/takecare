Meet Joel Preece

Take Care Property Maintenance isn't a faceless franchise. We are a proud, family-owned business deeply rooted in Rakaia, providing dedicated property services across the entire Ashburton District.

I started this business because I saw a genuine need for tradespeople who simply do what they say they are going to do. When you own a home or a lifestyle block, the maintenance list never truly ends. It can be stressful trying to find someone reliable to handle it.

That's where we step in. From clearing stubborn lichen off your roof to pouring a fresh concrete driveway, our approach is straightforward: we communicate clearly, we turn up on time, and we get the job done properly.

If we come across a highly specialized task outside our scope, our contractor networking promise means we will help you find a trusted local professional who can handle it. We believe in honest, transparent service from start to finish.
